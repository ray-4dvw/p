About 4D

In 2018, floorplans will become obsolete and homebuyers will be introduced to the 4th Dimension. Every pre-construction home will be rendered into 4D’s immersive real time 3D platform. No longer will you make the single most financial decision of your lifetime on a static floorplan.
Welcome to the 4D platform where homebuyers can walk through their exact rendered space, click and drag scalable furniture, purchase furniture, save their furniture onto the 4D Design Board, interact with all professionals in the real estate industry.
